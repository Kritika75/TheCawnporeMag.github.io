Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have question in mind ?

Know everything about The Cawnpore magazines
Q: Who can contribute?

A: Anyone with an interest in writing, art, or photography can contribute.

Q: What kind of content do you publish?

A: We publish a variety of content including poetry, prose, essays, visual art, and photography. Our aim is to celebrate diverse cultures through literature and art.

Q: How often do you publish new issues?

A: We publish new issues periodically. Keep an eye on our website and social media channels for announcements and release dates.

Q: Do you accept international submissions?

A: Yes, we are a global platform and accept submissions from creators around the world.

Q: What is the deadline for the submission?

A: Deadlines vary based on upcoming issues. Keep an eye on our website or social media pages for official announcements and submission windows.

Q: Do I retain rights to my work?

A: Yes! Contributors retain full rights to their work. By submitting, you only grant us permission to publish it on The Cawnpore Magazine platform (web and PDF formats).

Q: Can I share content from your magazine on my social media?

A: Yes, you are welcome to share our content. We just ask that you credit "The Cawnpore Magazine" and the original creator of the work.

Q: How to get involved as a developer or designer?

A: We’re open to collaboration! You can contribute to our open-source website on GitHub by fixing bugs, improving UI, or suggesting new features. Check out the GitHub Repository to get started with the contributing guidelines.
